About Me

My journey into cybersecurity started with a simple question — how do things break, and how do we stop them?

I pursued that curiosity all the way to an MCA in Cybersecurity, and today I work across both red and blue team domains — hunting for SQLi and XSS vulnerabilities, crafting exploit scripts in Python and Bash, and digging through Splunk logs to catch threats before they escalate.

I'm CPBT certified with deep knowledge of the CEH V13 curriculum, and I've built hands-on expertise across the full attack and defense lifecycle — from Nmap reconnaissance to malware reverse engineering. I also bring research experience as an AI & ML Researcher at Zoho, where I developed a strong understanding of intelligent systems — a perspective I now apply to anomaly detection and AI-driven threat analysis.

I'm at the start of my career, but I bring the mindset of someone who never stops learning.

Open to freelance engagements and full-time opportunities.
